About

When you find something that gives you sense of purpose, that’s when you’ve found something for you. But, for a while I struggled to find what was for me. I was always bored, and never able to put my creativity to the test. I loved fine arts, and design but after awhile I felt contained and needed a different way to elevate my creative thoughts, but I wasn’t sure how. “Web Development” I thought, why didn’t I think of this sooner.

Well... in my mind I never thought of programming as something very “fun” or “creative”, so why would I explore that realm. It wasn’t until my entire perspective was changed, I could combine both worlds.I could stand out or be a valuable asset to the world of programming. I became very interested in learning more.Imagine the most famous paintings, doing more than just existing! If people are satisfied with the art itself, what if I added more to it? Then what would happen?

So functionality - a specific purpose, programming seems to be the missing piece to my puzzle or the last piece to the bridge. Today I am in IT at NSCC studying to become a web developer. I hope to learn what it takes to become a web developer and help shape the future of the ever evolving web.
